We Felt the World Was Ours

By: Wizza and In'am Hanim

We are proud to bring you this podcast by Wizza and In'am Hanim. Consisting of two parts – each in Arabic and English – Wizza and In'am take listeners on a deep dive into the ongoing political, emotional, and familial impacts of the Egyptian revolution of 2011 and the events that followed on the young people who were children and teenagers at the time.
